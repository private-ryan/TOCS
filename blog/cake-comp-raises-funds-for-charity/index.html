Cake Comp Raises Funds for Charity

25 Jun 2012 / No Comments

We’re pleased to announce we raised £25 for Macmillan Cancer Support, from our “Guess the weight of the cake” competition, as part of The World’s Biggest Coffee Morning event, on the 30 September last year. Members made a small donation to enter the competition. The laptop inspired cake was won by Paul Tagent, Director of Bath Marketing Consultancy; who guessed the correct weight. We’d like to thank all the members of The Old Church School who joined in, and made the day a fun filled event. Macmillan Cancer Support provides practical, medical and financial support to those living with cancer. Last year 43,000 people signed up to hold an event, raising over £8 million. Paul kindly donated the cake to everyone at TOCS and within 2 days the 1.77 kg cake was scoffed!
