For solopreneurs, creatives, therapists, employees, managers…. and everything in between!

By rebecca / 28 Feb 2013 / No Comments

If you’re in Somerset, PASSIONfruit, is a new women’s network with a difference – and its not just for suit-wearing business types. If you’ve got a project on the go, or are out in the world doing your thing, getting together with other dynamic women is a must for keeping your cup full.

You can register here (the event is free, but PASSIONfruit will be taking donations for a different women’s charity each month – details will be released nearer the time), you’ll receive the full program and some handy tips on how to network if you feel you fancy a look-see.

“PASSIONfruit enables women to air the issues which concern them, find others with similar experience, tap potential solutions quickly – and make really genuine, lasting connections that nourish their business.” Alice Irving, the event organiser.

The first event is taking place Friday 8 March 9-11am at Diva’s Cafe, Frome. Check out more info here

 

Alice Irving specialises in coaching and consulting for authentic business and fulfilled living. She is a former London-based Arts Consultant, Alice now runs The Hand Made Life, providing inspiration and support to design your own unique approach to life and work and get things done your way.
