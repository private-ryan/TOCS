Only for the brave!!

By rebecca / 16 Jan 2013 / No Comments

Need an excuse for a way to shift those last few stubborn pounds gained over the Christmas break? Frome Triathlon Club, in association with Hot Chilli Tri Club, are running a winter aquathlon (swim+run) series fittingly called ‘The Damp Dash’. Dates are  Sunday 31 March at Frome Leisure Centre and 10 February at Trowbridge Pool.The event is open to all abilities and there is also a Junior entry;  for children aged 8 years and above. For more information on these events or an entry form please contact Mark Poole at markgpoole@me.com, there are various entry categories and prices to choose from. Let us know if you had a go!!
