Do you feel you need to start working on your business, as well as working in your business?

By rebecca / 02 Oct 2012 / No Comments

If you feel that you need to take a look at how your business is doing and create some new goals for the New Year ahead, then this workshop could be for you! Andy Jackson will be running a workshop on 17  October at Rudloe Hall Hotel, Corsham. It’s a morning that will help you to develop some ideas in the following:

  • What you want your business to have achieved by the end of 2013
  • What you want to have achieved by the end of this year
  • How to ensure you’re focusing on the right tasks and activities for your business
  • The things you should consider getting someone else to do – or don’t bother doing at all!
  •  Identifying your ideal customer
  •  Developing new ways to attract enquiries
  • How to make the most of marketing methods that will work best for your business

The morning workshop will run from 8.45am with coffee, tea & bacon butties and finish at 1.00pm, £47 per person. Bargain!

And of course, there will be a whole bunch of other like-minded people there – so it’s a great opportunity to share some advice and learn about other businesses in the area.

To reserve a place call Andy on 07789 866586 or contact him at aj@andyjackson.org He’s a really lovey guy and genuinely enjoys helping people move their businesses further.
