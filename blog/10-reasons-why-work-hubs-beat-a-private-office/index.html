10 Reasons Why Work Hubs Beat A Private Office…

By rebecca / 25 Jun 2012 / No Comments

1.Co-working offers pay as you go tariffs: Most office rentals are expensive and tie you into a long term lease. Co-working offers a no obligation approach to working. Affordable and flexible tariffs where you can chop and change month by month to suit your needs. Ideal for freelancers and small businesses. And you can simply borrow all the office stuff, no need to fork out on furniture, utilities or WiFi, it’s a package deal!

2.It’s so Social: In a private office, you can shut the door on the outside world and focus on the job at hand. But for some, this can be isolating and is a difficult part of running your own business. Work hubs can give you that social element without you having to make a huge effort, as the hustle and bustle of life is all around! Location is also key to the position of a work hub, so the community of the local town shouldn’t be too far from the doorstep!

3.Building friendships: When working around the same people day in day out, its inevitable people will form bonds and grab lunch or go out for drinks after the working day is done. Hubs that host events are also likely to bring people together and achieve a more intimately connected community. You can also get a sense of reassurance from others working around you and share any difficulties you are facing with tricky clients, a problem shared is a problem halved!

4.Pet friendly: Many co-working spaces allow pets and we can tell you from experience, that animals bring new life to the workplace! There’s something comforting about having pets around and they keep things fun and of course you’re always expecting the unexpected!

5.Meeting space: Though a private office gives you the ideal setting for confidential meeting space, many work hubs can offer meeting rooms that enable you to discuss business without interruption. It’s also good to invite your client to be part of the small business community in your local town/city and by introducing them into the world of co working; you could enable them to make contacts of their own.

6.Connect with professionals: Working in a co-working setting allows you to get to know people and experts in their field, or even get recommendations. It’s common for people to hire each other and even start up partnerships and companies together. Why? Because it’s all on your doorstep and you get to know and see how that company or person works. At TOCS we have people who collaborate on a daily basis and have helped each other further their business opportunities!

7.Free expert advice: Need feedback on your latest innovation? Having second thoughts about your company’s new logo?  There is always someone around to ask. Many work hubs also host events for local businesses, so you’ll always be in the know!

8.Free publicity: Often co-working spaces will promote their members to the local and broader community. TOCS has an enewsletter and a friends and clients page on our website, linking members to their websites and giving browsers an insight into the work they do. Social media now plays a big part in coworking spaces and is yet another platform to share and swap news.

9.Free space: In a private office, space is often limited, work hubs are spacious buildings and flexible in their approach to letting members use space. Often there will be a lounge, resource room or studio where you can meet clients or host events in a more relaxed atmosphere than a private self contained office rental.

10.A more productive day: Often being surrounded by people doing and things happening can spur you on to achieve your goals. It’s also pretty hard to daydream and stare off into space when you’re around others without someone asking “a penny for your thoughts?”

So there you have it, just a few reasons as to why we think it’s great to work in a co-working hub.
