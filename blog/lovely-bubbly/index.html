Lovely Bubbly!

By rebecca / 29 Jun 2012 / No Comments

At The Old Church School we’re always trying to do our bit to help the environment, so we’ve just installed water saving gizmos on our taps! We’re already noticing the difference…it doesn’t feel like we’re getting any less water, but it has obviously reduced the water flow and the water feels softer and more bubbly than before, “spa like” someone commented. Maybe now we’ll all have more youthful looking hands from our spa like bubbly water…probably not, but at least we’ve made a small difference!
