Support for High Growth SME’s

By rebecca / 29 Nov 2012 / No Comments

Forward space is holding a free breakfast with GrowthAccelerator; a new support service aimed at helping businesses achieve high growth and reach their potential with advice and guidance through business development coaching, funding opportunities and creating new products and service/s.

GrowthAccelerator is designed to support a wide variety of businesses looking to rapidly grow. It doesn’t matter what stage of growth you’re at, as long as you can complete the following check-list, they can help you: Is your company registered in England? Are you determined to grow your business? Is your turnover under £40m? Do you employ less than 250 people?

Growth Manager Mike Parry, will explain more about how you can get tailor made expert advice to achieve your business ambitions. One to one surgeries will follow after; these have to be booked in advance. The free breakfast event is open to anyone running a business or in the process of starting up.

Book a place at our breakfast event by emailing rebecca@forwardspace.co.uk you can also book your free one to one surgery with Mike Parry through Rebecca. We’re very excited about this new initiative – we hope you are too! There is no obligation to sign up to the service if you make an appointment!

23rd January 2013. The breakfast starts 8am and runs til 9am, after which appointments follow.
