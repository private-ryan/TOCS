Great opportunity for freelance java/database/web developer

By rebecca / 09 Nov 2012 / 4 Comments

Would this be of interest to you or anyone you know? Great opportunity for freelance java/database/web developer approx 2 days per week for at least next 6 months.

Kit Kilne runs a technology consultancy business offering: IT strategy and planning, Technical architecture, systems engineering and delivery management and Bespoke application development and support. With a strong focus on non-profit organisations but also work in the private and public sector.

Kit is looking to form a long term relationship with a freelance developer to provide extra capacity for current application development contracts. The volume of work will fluctuate but be of the order of 2 days per week for at least the next 6 months. You will be able to work remotely on all jobs but must be prepared to occasionally travel to my offices near Bath and work on-site there for a day including meetings. You must therefore be a UK resident.

Brief summery of terms:
  • Freelance contract: Although I is not looking to directly employ staff one of the pleasures for me, in work, is forming long term relationships with good people and a long-term freelance association would work well
  • The work will be primarily on a specific international client with a legacy Java Web App that needs ongoing support and development – but I also have a need for some capacity on other jobs.
  •   I would initially manage contact with clients but, over time, as we get to know each other better, direct contact with clients will be more efficient and effective
  •   The list of required skills can be found below. You must have the essential skills to a high level and with a number of years’ experience. Where a ‘nice to have’ is required that is outside of your skill set you must be prepared to gain those skills ‘on your own time’
  •  Mixture (depending on the specific job) of fixed price outcome tasks and timesheet- based day rate work
Personality required:
  • Most importantly you need to be a highly competent and experienced developer – a self-starter, good at working independently and solving issues, You will have a number of years’ experience and have current work you could describe or show me.
  •  Good communication and customer skills are essential so that, after an introductory period, I can have you work directly with clients.
Technical skills required:
  •  Essential: Java and Java Web Applications
  •   Essential: XML and XML Schema
  •  Essential: Unit testing (Junit ideally)
  •  Essential: Source code management (Git ideally)
  •   Essential: IDE tools for development, debug of java and java applications (ideallyEclipse)
  •   Essential: Web UI (HTML4, basic Javascript)
  •   Nice to have: high level JavaScript, JQuery, JQueryUI, HTML5
  •   Nice to have: App development (android/apple)
  •  Nice to have: Experience of Maven for dependency and build management
  •   Nice to have: Tomcat application server

Please apply for this position ASAP.

Phone: +44 (0)7769 224896  or email: kit@kitkline.co.uk  check out his website  www.kitkline.co.uk for more info about the company.
