Need some guidance about how to invoice for your work and keep up to date with finances?

By rebecca / 26 Oct 2012 / No Comments

We’re hosting a free workshop which will cover what is needed when invoicing clients for work, as well as many financial obligations and considerations needed when running a business providing services; as a freelancer or small business owner. Barbara Bacon, who has over 17 years experience as a bookkeeper working with small businesses in the Frome area will be covering the following in this informative workshop:

Invoice and Sales

Order requirements

Knowing your clients/customers

Credit control

Invoice/financing/factoring

County Court money claims

This free workshop will run on 29 November at The Old Church School, Butts Hill, Frome. 7pm-8.30pm. Please make sure you book your place here as previous workshops with Barbara have been very popular. If you cannot make the workshop, please email rebecca@forwardspace.co.uk
