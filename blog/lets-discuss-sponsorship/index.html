Let’s Discuss Sponsorship

28 Sep 2012 / No Comments

Discuss and Do is a series of workshops, talks and social events aimed at businesses in the South West, running from January to July 2013. The festival seeks to bring together business, innovation, technology and creativity. There are seven discussion topics, with up to 3 events per topic.

The topics are: Women in Business, Social Media, Finance, Branding Marketing and Design, Selling, Getting Started and Under 25s. There are several options for sponsorship, each providing different levels of exposure for your organisation. The options are:

Tier 1- £1000 Global Sponsorship (one only) Global Sponsorship of the entire festival, including all topics and events. This is a great opportunity to enhance your business profile via the following:

  • Your logo on the Global Sponsor page of the Discuss and Do website, as the only commercial Global Sponsor (alongside Frome Chamber of Commerce, Somerset County Council & The Old Church School)
  • Free seats and invitations for you and up to 5 guests to the event/s of your choice
  • Continued reference to your involvement throughout the festival’s duration
  • Inclusion in any marketing or public relations collateral
  • Your logo and a link to your website will appear on each of the e-flyers sent out
  •  Your business will be thanked at the beginning or end of each talk

Tier 2- £250 Discussion Topic Sponsorship (Sole Sponsor) Sole sponsorship of one of the seven discussion topics, providing a great opportunity to increase visibility. Tier 2 sponsorship includes:

  • Your business will be thanked at the beginning or end of the talk Your logo and a link to your website will appear in the topic page on the Discuss and Do website
  • Your logo and a link to your website will appear in the e-flyer for your chosen topic
  • Free seats and invitations for you and up to 2 guests to the talk of your chosen topic

Tier 3- £100 Discussion Topic Sponsorship (Co-Sponsor) Co-sponsorship of one of the seven discussion topics along with 2 other Co-Sponsors. Tier 3 sponsorship includes:

  • Your business will be thanked at the beginning or end of the talk
  •  Your logo and a link to your website will appear in the topic page on the Discuss and Do website
  • Your logo and a link to your website will appear in the e-flyer for your chosen topic
  • Free seats and invitations for you and a plus one to the talk of your chosen topic

If you have any questions or would like to sponsor Discuss and Do, please contact the team at: talk@discussanddo.co.uk
