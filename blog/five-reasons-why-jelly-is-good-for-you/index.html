Five Reasons Why Jelly Is Good For You!

By rebecca / 25 Jun 2012 / 1 Comment

1. Jelly is fun: Jelly is not a “networking” event. You don’t pitch your business, or meet new clients. It’s informal, where you can meet up with like-minded people in a relaxed environment and carry on with work. Jelly is great for freelancers, home workers and small business owners, as it gives you the opportunity to meet others who work in the same way and face the same challenges.

2. Jelly is a change of scene:  Working from home has many benefits for the self employed: flexibility, increased motivation and can prove useful for working parents with childcare responsibilities. However, occasionally separating yourself from the day to day routine and the juggling of work/home commitments can be beneficial to your business. Jelly can be a bit of light relief, whether you choose to attend every month or just dip in and out when it suits you. That’s the beauty of Jelly!

3. Jelly can bring new ideas to the co-working table: Working alongside new and other talented professionals can open up a new source for ideas. More ideas and seeing something from another angle can be useful if you are working for yourself.

4. It’s free: Jelly is a free event, all you need to bring is yourself, your phone and your laptop and that’s it! WiFi, refreshments and the fun don’t cost you a penny!

5. Jelly can take you places:  Because Jelly is a  of work and chat, talk often leads to sharing ideas, tips and sometimes starting or collaborating on a new project. You can also find out where things are happening in your town, opening up more opportunities for you and your business.

So, if you feel Jelly sounds like it could be for you, get in touch! We have a monthly Jelly here at TOCS, third Wednesday of the month!
