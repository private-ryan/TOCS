Why don’t you design a hat for Aurora St Clare…

By rebecca / 22 Jun 2012 / No Comments

Frome’s ‘Splash of Purple Day’, organised by members of Positive Action on Cancer, has been branded a great success over the last few years and this year is set to be just as successful. We thought we’d join in the fun this year, along with Truly Sopel, local fashion designer and held a creative competition, which successfully helped to raise money and awareness for the charity. Design a Hat for Aurora St Clare launched Monday the 30 January 2012.

  • 1st prize-was an Abernethy’s Cut & blow dry, hair treatments and a goody bag of after-care products!
  • 2nd prize-a Sunday lunch for two @The Cornerhouse, Frome and a purple Bodum blade grinder, 3 cup cafeteire with coffee beans donated by Emporio La Strada!
  • 3rd prize-a hamper of sparkly trinkets and treats, provided by many local businesses! The final designd were chosen by the Frome famous mannequin Aurora St Clare herslef! Our wonderful sponsors were: Emporio La Strada Abernethy’s hairdressing Deadly is the Female Truly Knickers Droollery, and The Cornerhouse, Frome. We had great entries for this PAC inspired hat comp and choosing the final three was hard work indeed!

Winners were announced on 2 of March, to coincide with the splash of purple annual event! You can check out the pictures from this event here!
