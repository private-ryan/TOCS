Women in Business

By rebecca / 28 Jan 2013 / No Comments

Thank you to everyone who attended our first event as part of the Discuss and Do business festival. We really enjoyed the first topic ‘Women in Business’Â  (25/01/13) and the insightful contributions Margaret Heffernan shared in the event. The lunch was a full of great conversation and Margaret showed her experience and business aptitude with advice and conversation throughout. The talk was just as inspiring – with great questions form the audience. Margaret demonstrated her wealth of knowledge about the subject and how women bring a more emotional intelligence to the business community of today. We feel very fortunate to have had someone of Margaret’s professional expertise and credentials involved in the festival and we’re looking forward to the publication of her next book! Our favourite advice over lunch was concerning the delegation to staff when running a small business and how if you don’t trust your employees to do their job then “your business will never be smarter than you are!”
