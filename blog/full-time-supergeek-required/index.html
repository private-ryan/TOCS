Full Time Supergeek Required!

By rebecca / 31 Mar 2013 / No Comments

Due to expansion iPhoneLab require a person who has surpassed the super geek state of being! This is a fantastic opportunity if you have an interest in technology and an aptitude for de-constructing and assembling mobile communication devices. Full training will be given, but a strong focus to provide high quality technical expertise and support to their ever expanding client base is a must!

Other skills required:

Time management and organisational skills
Honest, good humoured and hard-working
The ability to communicate technical information to a non-technical audience

iPhoneLab provides quality iPhone colour customisation, iPhone repairs and iPad Repairs. Please email a CV, Covering letter and a joke to hello@iphonelab.co.uk Or send your CV; CL and joke to:

iPhoneLab

The Old Church School

Butts Hill

Frome BA11 1HR

Salary and benefits are by negotiation

This role is a full-time permanent position  – based on a 39 hour week. Flexible working hours are available to fit around child care etc. Closing date is 10 April.
