Pop Up TOCS Curry Club

By rebecca / 17 Jan 2013 / No Comments
We’re hosting a Curry Club this month, catered by the brilliant Old Bakery Supper Club. It will be here at TOCS and is open to anyone who’d like to come along! It will be £12 p/head (BYOB) which will include a starter and main, of course there’ll be a vegetarian option available. We’ll be sitting down for our starters at 7:30, so make sure you’re here a little before to say Hello! and grab a seat.

The menu is as follows:

Starter: Onion bhajis with a yoghurt/mint sauce and an Asian spiced salad.

Main course: Kofta and Rogan Josh (vegetarian versions of both available). Each person will receive a serving of both, so that the warmer Rogan Josh compliments the milder Kofta.

When: 31 January, 7PM
Where: The Old Church School, Butts Hill, Frome, BA11 1HR 
How Much: £12

If you would like a space, please let us know by contacting our lovely prefect Ryan ryan@forwardspace.co.uk
