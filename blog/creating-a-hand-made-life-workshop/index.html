Creating a Hand Made Life Workshop

By ryan / 07 Sep 2012 / No Comments

Have you ever found yourself at the end of another exhausting day thinking: “there’s got to be a better way of doing this”?

Everyone deserves to leap out of bed in the morning with a sense of excitement and anticipation.

If your daily smorgasbord of activities isn’t quite hitting the mark, perhaps it’s time to take a step back and regroup.

Create your very own Hand Made Life.

Former London-based Arts Consultant Alice Irving runs The Hand Made Life, providing inspiration and support to design your own unique approach to life and work and get things done your way.

Her approach combines insightful thinking with ruthlessly practical how-to, using a range of tools from the tried and tested to the cutting edge.

By you. For you. Made to Measure.

Whether its work/life balance, a project you’re struggling with, a dream you need to realise or a renewed sense of direction, taking some time out to regroup and skill up is going to be invaluable to set you on your way with confidence and potency.

12 hours to skill up for a Hand Made Life.

Alice is running a 4 week workshop at The Old Church School in September for people who want to find out more about her way of working and learn some killer techniques for creating change.

A jam packed series of 4 morning sessions gives you the nuts and bolts you need to manage yourself, think about what you want and actually get it done, in a work setting or at home.

Each week will cover a different stage of the process from Vision, Plan, Troubleshoot to Deliver, so that, by the end, you’ll have a tool bag of skills to draw on for years to come.

Participants get a 10% discount on one-to-one coaching too.

Starts Saturday 15th September.

In the fabulously funky Lounge of the Old Church School, with a proper coffee machine and shelves of inspiring and intriguing books to browse in the breaks.

Last few places remaining. Booking Essential.

More information at www.thehandmadelife.co.uk/handmadehelp/workshops
