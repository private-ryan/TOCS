Online accounting can be fun!

21 Jun 2012 / 1 Comment

We had a great online accounting workshop with Nigel Harris, Partner at Burton Sweet on 14 March.  Nigel talked us through some of the software and packages available for accounting, and gave a thorough demonstration of all the clever things  Xero (online accounting software) can do-ideal for a small business. Nigel really knew his stuff and was great at gearing the workshop to suit the needs of attendees, whether they were a market trader, bookkeeper or  a freelancer, Nigel could point us all in the right direction, with how Xero best suited each business. The workshop highlighted how important accounts are for any business, and how simple Xero is to use and can help you to take control of your business finances; giving an insight into how a business is doing and how this can help to manage the business in the future. Nigel was funny, warm and engaging and even made online accounting fun and exciting!  The talk emphasized the many advantages of ‘cloud’ accounting – especially for those working remotely, from home or anyone in a partnership. We’d like to thank Nigel for a great workshop and recommend him and Burton Sweet for small businesses who’d like help, advice or the services Burton Sweet can offer.
