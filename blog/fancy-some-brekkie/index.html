Fancy some brekkie?

By rebecca / 25 Jun 2012 / No Comments

We had another scrummy breakfast at Divas Cafe! “Business for Breakfast” an informal business get-together, on the 8 March. The event included a breakfast buffet for £6.50 per head. With Bacon and free range egg  with chilli Jam, Veggie sausage and egg butty with chilli jam, Homemade Granola, Greek yoghurt and honey, Strawberry, banana and mango smoothie and Tea or coffee. Yum! Divas cafe is situated in the Black Swan Arts in the town centre; the café has full disabled access. The event will run from 8.30am-9.45am. For anyone who is interested in this event, please book your place by contacting Rebecca on 01373 888 108 or emailing host@forwardspace.co.uk
