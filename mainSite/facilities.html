We are open from 8.00am to 6.00pm Monday to Friday.

If you are on our full-time Open Space tariff we give you your own key and alarm tag for 24/7 access.

The Old Church School offers more than just work space. We have a range of services designed to make your life easier.

What we've got?

  • A town centre location in a beautiful grade II listed building 
  • 24Mb broadband (wireless and Cat5E wired network) 
  • VoIP telephone system 
  • Meeting rooms with lime green Eames chairs (you gotta see 'em!)
  • Digital projectors and flip charts (dried out marker pens optional)
  • Recycling facilities, waste collection and even our own compost bin
  • Sexy bike racks (we designed them ourselves)
  • A very expensive coffee machine supplying amazing Mozzo coffee
  • A kitchen and lounge for informal gatherings
  • Free car parking (for you and your visitors)
  • Desks designed for us by Oliver Marlow at TILT
  • Full colour A3 printer, photocopier, fax and scanner
  • Secure storage
  • Central heating with individual thermostats
  • Windows that open
  • Disabled access and a disabled toilet
  • Document shredding
  • Shared resource library (feel free to bring your own books to add to it)
  • Comfy sofas
  • Advice and support services (plus a shoulder to cry on)
  • Regular Business Link clinics
  • Regular coworking events (such as Jelly)
  • Friendly staff to help you and ensure everything is running smoothly
  • IT support through AF-IT
  • Fully alarmed building with security shutters on windows
  • A walk-in safe (used to securely stow mops and cleaning products)
  • Mail Boxes
  • Virtual PA facilities

What we haven't got?

  • Attitude
  • Air-conditioning.  That's why windows were invented
  • A location on an out of town business park (sorry it's just not our thing)
  • Slippers (but please feel free to bring your own)
