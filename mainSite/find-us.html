Find Us

The Old Church School
Butts Hill
Frome
Somerset
BA11 1HR*

Find us using this map or follow these simple directions

From the centre of Frome, head to the roundabout at the top of Bath Street, and turn right onto Christchurch Street West (A362)

Take the second left - Park Road (opposite the Memorial Theatre)

Head up Park Road and after 50m turn left down a narrow lane.  If you are a visual person look for our lovely lime green sign.  If you are numerical in inclination, count four trees on the left hand side of Park Road and the lane is immediately after the fourth tree!  We are at the far end of the lane

*Sat Nav Users - our postcode will take you to the pedestrian entrance (tricky if you are in a car!).  If you end up a little lost, head down the hill to the roundabout, keep left onto Christchurch Street West and follow the instructions above

Parking

We have our own free car park immediately adjacent to the building

By Bus

The nearest bus stops are the Memorial Theatre and the Fire Station

By Bike

We have secure bike racks in front of the building

By Train

Frome train station is about a 5 minute walk.  Westbury, Warminster and Bath are all within a 20 minute drive
