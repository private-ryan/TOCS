Open Plan Coworking

It couldn't be simpler - you decide how often you need to be here, what facilities you need and what your budget is and match your needs with one of our flexible tariffs.

The next step is to sign up for membership.

Done? Excellent. Now simply use our online booking system to book desks or meeting rooms whenever suits you.

You can book up to 6 desks at a time - so for eg. if you book 3 desks on Monday for 1 hour (3 x 1 =3hrs), and 1 desk on Tuesday for 6 hours (1 x 6 =6hrs) we will deduct 9 hours from the time remaining on your tariff.  You can use the time remaining whenever you like in that calendar month - you choose when and how you would like to use it.

If you would like to have a full-time office with us then sign up for our Open Space tariff.  This is priced per person - but we give you big discounts for each extra person you add (up to a maximum of 6).  See our tariffs.

From month to month you can change tariff to take account of how much time you want to spend here.  Each month we send you a single fully-itemised bill covering everything.  And that's it.

We're all about flexibility and making things easy for you, so if you've got any questions about signing up, the facilities available, what tariff suits you, how the coffee machine works - just  ask, we like helping people.

Studio Offices

We also have a number of studio offices on the first floor of the building.  These are loft-style with oak floors, huge windows, high ceilings and exposed rafters.  They vary in size from 4-10 people.  They also share the other facilities in the building including meeting rooms, the lounge and kitchen.
